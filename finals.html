Term Reflection

For our finalterm, the first thing we learned about was AI assistants and chatbots. A chatbot is a system or program designed to mimic or to simulate human conversation with users. Typically they are powered by advanced NLP models like Text Generation Models and other Large Language Models (LLMs). These models are advanced and they are capable of understanding and interpreting human language in order to provide answers. Chatbots are widely used in various domains, assisting users with frequently asked questions or any other questions that is related to their specific fields.

One example of a Large Language Model that is popular today is GPT, which stands for Generative Pre-trained Transformer. Developed by OpenAI, an AI research and deployment company focused on benefiting humans through artificial intelligence, GPT is capable of generating human like conversations and performing different tasks like summarization and translation. GPT provides answers using a probabilistic approach, which does not guarantee correct responses and this phenomenon is called hallucination.

We also learned how to fine-tune a pre-trained model. Fine-tuning involves taking a pre-trained model and training it on a smaller scale or for a specific task to improve its performance in that field or domain.

The difference between a pre-trained model and a fine-tuned model is that the pre-trained model serves as the basic and fundamental structure trained on large datasets. Then in the fine-tuned model, it is an optimized version designed for a specific task. Typically, task-specific data is fed into the pre-trained model to optimize its performance for that task. Fine-tuning is done to enhance performance in a particular area or domain.
